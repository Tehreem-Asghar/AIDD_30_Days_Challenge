## AIDD 30-Day Challenge — Task 1 Solution

# 1. What does AI-Driven Development mean to me?

AI-Driven Development ka matlab hai ke AI humari coding aur software development me Pair/helper ban kar kaam kare. Ye October 2025 ke AI Turning Point ke baad aur important ho gaya, jab AI tools itne smart ho gaye ke wo code generate kar sakein hai hamri machine ma aur developers ke kaam ko fast aur accurate bana sakein. AI repetitive tasks aur code generation sambhalta hai, aur hum insaan strategy, planning aur creative solutions par focus karte hain.

## 2. How do I see the future of Human–AI Collaboration?

Future me AI aur insaan mil kar kaam karenge. AI time-consuming tasks like coding karega aur insaan apni creativity aur decision-making par kaam karega like write specification about project . Dono mil kar zyada tezz aur smart solutions bana sakenge. Human–AI collaboration se developers ka role evolve hoga, sirf coder se strategic problem solver banega.
